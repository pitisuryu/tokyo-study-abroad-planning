# Walking Directions: Tokyo Station → Tokyo Tower

1. Exit Tokyo Station via the Marunouchi North Exit or Yaesu Central Exit, heading west on Eitai‑dori Avenue (toward Yurakucho).
2. Continue straight for 800 m, passing Yurakucho Station on your left. Turn right onto Shinbashi‑dori Avenue (after about 1 km total).
3. Walk 1.2 km south on Shinbashi‑dori, crossing Shimbashi Station. Continue straight as it becomes Harumi‑dori Avenue.
4. After 1.5 km, turn left onto Shibadaimon‑dori, then slight right toward Zojo‑ji Temple area.
5. Proceed 800 m to Tokyo Tower Main Entrance (4‑2‑8 Shiba‑Koen, Minato City). Landmarks: Zojo‑ji Temple and the orange Tokyo Tower visible ahead.
